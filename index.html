About Pawan Kalyan

Pawan Kalyan, fondly known as the Power Star, is one of the most celebrated actors and influential politicians in Telugu cinema and Andhra Pradesh politics. Known for his charismatic presence and strong screen persona, he has inspired millions through his films and his leadership.

Apart from being a dynamic actor, Pawan Kalyan founded the Jana Sena Party in 2014 and continues to be a voice of the people, advocating for justice, equality, and reform.
